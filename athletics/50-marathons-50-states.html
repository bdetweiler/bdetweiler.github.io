50 States Marathon Club

The 50 States Marathon Club is pretty self explanitory. Run a marathon (or greater) in all 50 states in your lifetime.

The rules are fairly straightforward, but basically boil down to signing up for and running a legitimate marathon or ultramarathon within the allotted cutoff. There must be at least 10 finishers, and races run across state borders can only cound toward one state.
