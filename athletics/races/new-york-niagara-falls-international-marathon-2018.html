Recap

My training had not been quite as strong leading into this marathon. The biggest issue I faced were my shoes, which turned out to be too narrow, causing severe blisters.

This race hurt quite a bit. When I reached the Falls, I could barely appreciate the beauty because of the pain I was in. Things could have gone better.
